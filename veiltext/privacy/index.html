Veiltext Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Short version: Veiltext does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

Local Storage Only

All notes and data created in Veiltext are stored locally on your device. Veiltext does not use any external servers, cloud services, or databases. Your notes never leave your device.

Encryption

Notes are encrypted on-device before being stored. The encryption key is generated and stored on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere. Even if someone gains access to your device storage, your notes cannot be read without the key.

Permissions

Veiltext may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage — required to save and read your notes on-device.
  • Biometric / Face ID / Touch ID — optional, used only for app unlock if you choose to enable it.

No other permissions are requested. Veiltext does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other data.

Analytics & Tracking

Veiltext does not use any analytics, crash reporting SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. No usage data is ever collected or sent anywhere.

Purchases

Any in-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Veiltext does not receive or store payment information. For details on how purchase data is handled, refer to the respective platform's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Veiltext does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that could collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Veiltext does not knowingly collect any information from children. Because no data is collected from any user, Veiltext is safe for all ages in this regard.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL. The "last updated" date at the top of the page will reflect any changes.
